👨‍💻 Tech & Public Affairs Professionnal

My responsibility consisted in enhancing bilateral cooperation on political and ethical issues of innovation, and rolling out a strategy of online influence for the French Institute of Estonia.

Not sure what France can bring to a pioneer country like Estonia? Take 5mn to read about:

  • 👉 the role of France at the 2017 Tallinn Digital Summit

Working for the French Ministry of Foreign Affairs in the most digitally avanced nation that is Estonia made me learn a lot about the future of institutions, democracy and diplomacy.

📢 Open Government Advocate

I co-founded in 2015 the French chapter of DemocracyOS,

a global movement that promotes open source civic tech for more inclusive models of governance.

DemocracyOS France is now a leading actor of f0rk (ex- Open Democracy Now), the most effervescent activist network in France dedicated to promoting digital commons in democratic innovation.

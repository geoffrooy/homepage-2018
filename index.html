I co-founded in 2015 the French chapter of DemocracyOS,

a global movement that promotes open source civic tech for more inclusive models of governance.

DemocracyOS France is now a leading actor of Open Democracy Now, the most effervescent activist network in France dedicated to promoting digital commons in democratic innovation.
